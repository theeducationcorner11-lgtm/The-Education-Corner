About

We offer online 11+ tutoring for Maths, English, Verbal Reasoning, and Non-Verbal Reasoning. All lessons are conducted on Zoom, and homework is shared via Google Classroom.

Subjects

English

Non-Verbal Reasoning

Each week includes 1 lesson covering Maths, Verbal, and Non-Verbal. English is repeating the same skills (don't worry, this works!). Homework and extra practice are on Google Classroom.
